About Our College

Our college, established in 1962, is dedicated to providing quality education and fostering holistic development. With a wide range of academic programs, experienced faculty, and a vibrant campus life, we empower students to achieve excellence and become responsible citizens.

Our Mission

To nurture talent, encourage innovation, and contribute to the betterment of society by providing a holistic educational experience.

Our Vision

To be a leading institution recognized for academic excellence, research, and community engagement.

Principal's Message

"At My College, we believe in empowering our students with knowledge, skills, and values to face the challenges of the modern world. Welcome to a journey of learning and growth."

Admissions

Admission Process

Admissions for the academic year 2025-26 are now open! Interested candidates can apply online through our official website. The process includes filling out the application form, submitting required documents, and attending counseling sessions as per the schedule.

Eligibility Criteria

  • For undergraduate courses: Completion of 10+2 or equivalent from a recognized board.
  • For postgraduate courses: Graduation in the relevant stream from a recognized university.
  • Specific eligibility may vary by course. Please refer to the course details for more information.

Important Dates

  • Application Start Date: 1st May 2025
  • Application End Date: 30th June 2025
  • Merit List Announcement: 10th July 2025
  • Counseling & Admission: 15th - 25th July 2025

For more details, visit the Admissions Portal or contact our admissions office.
